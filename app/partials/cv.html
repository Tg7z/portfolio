What am I

I am Front-end / UI developer with 2 years experience and a supporting knowledge of the design process and terminology. I hand code semantic W3C validated markup that works across all major browsers without resorting to hacks.

I have a keen interest in crafting intuitive and rich user experiences and am very intrigued by the potential of next generation client-side MV* frameworks.

I would like to be a team member in a creative challenging and collaborative environment, where my passion and knowledge of front-end development and web-design will be well utilized, challenged and developed.
